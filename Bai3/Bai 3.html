One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in his life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he become acquainted with the inner city streets, ans realizies the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a change in the courts. the Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm. The Pelican Brief, and The Client.
